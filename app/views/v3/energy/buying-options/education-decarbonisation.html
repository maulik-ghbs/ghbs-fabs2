What it offers

Education decarbonisation framework provides a compliant route to secure specialist sector-specific carbon reduction expertise. The framework offers streamlined access to pre-approved suppliers and includes dedicated bid application support.

Benefits

  • quick and simple to access
  • set up specifically to support the education sector
  • access can be done on a ‘no-obligation’ basis

All frameworks also offer

  • compliance with the relevant procurement regulations
  • suppliers pre-checked by the approved framework provider
  • pre-agreed terms and conditions
